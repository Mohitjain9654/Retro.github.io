About Us

Who we are

Welcome to our project! We are second Year computer Science students, working on an college project, Our aim is to find solution for easy installation and cost effective for retrofitting ICE Vehicle to Electric vehicle.

Our Goal

Our Goal is to achieve retrofitting process affordable, and environmentally impactful. Our Aim is to contribute to a clenaner Planet by reducing Carbon Emission by providing user friendly solution.

What we do

Our Platform assessing vehicle conditions to recommending necessary upgrades, we provide step by step guidance to convert ICE vehicle to electric vehicle.
